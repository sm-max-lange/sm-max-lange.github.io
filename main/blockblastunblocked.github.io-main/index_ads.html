About Hills of Steel

Hills Of Steel is an exciting action-packed, side-scrolling tank game where you drive tanks over hilly landscapes, shoot enemies, and collect loot.

The core objective of Hills Of Steel is to defeat enemy tanks, vehicles, and bosses while navigating a challenging and dynamic terrain. Each battle takes place in a different environment, adding depth and variety to the game. This free-to-play online game combines thrilling physics-based mechanics with simple yet engaging gameplay.

The Features

Hills Of Steel comes with a range of exciting features that keep players hooked:

  • Variety of Tanks: Players can choose from several different tanks, each with unique abilities and characteristics.
  • Upgrades: Tanks can be upgraded with better weapons, armor, and engines to increase their combat effectiveness.
  • Physics-Based Combat: The game’s physics engine adds a layer of complexity and fun to the gameplay. The weight and movement of the tanks, the trajectory of the shots, and the terrain’s slopes and curves all affect how battles play out.

How to Play Hills Of Steel

Hills Of Steel Gameplay

Playing Hills Of Steel is simple and intuitive. The player controls a tank by moving it forward and backward while using a fire button to shoot at enemies. However, the key challenge lies in mastering the game's physics. The hilly terrain can slow down the player’s tank or make it difficult to aim at enemies, adding a strategic element to the combat.

Each tank comes with different stats for speed, firepower, and durability, so understanding how to maneuver your tank in different situations is crucial. Players must also take care to balance aggressive play with defense, using the hills as cover or to gain a height advantage.

Tips and Strategies

  • Upgrade Wisely: Focus on upgrading your tank’s firepower early in the game to gain an advantage in battles. Later, invest in armor and engine upgrades to ensure your tank is fast and durable.
  • Master the Terrain: Use the hills to your advantage. Positioning your tank on a slope can give you better firing angles or provide cover from enemy fire. Always keep an eye on the landscape.
  • Choose the Right Tank: Some tanks are better suited for certain levels or game modes. Experiment with different tanks to find the one that fits your playstyle and the current challenge.

Controls

  • Move: use the A and D keys or the Arrow keys to drive your tank!
  • Shoot: use the spacebar to shoot!
  • Repair: press the B button to repair your tank

Similar Games
