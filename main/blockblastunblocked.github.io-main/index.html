How to Play

The core of Block Blast is incredibly simple to grasp, making it easy for anyone to pick up and play:

  • Block Presentation: At the bottom of the screen, you'll see a selection of three different shaped blocks. These blocks are composed of varying numbers of squares arranged in unique configurations.
  • The Grid: The main playing area is a 8x8 grid. This is where you'll be placing your blocks.
  • Creating Lines: The objective is to create complete horizontal or vertical lines of blocks. Once a line is filled, it disappears to fill the space.
  • Game Over: The game ends when you run out of space to place any of the presented blocks.

Tips for Mastering Block Blast

To truly excel at Block Blast and achieve high scores, consider these advanced strategies:
